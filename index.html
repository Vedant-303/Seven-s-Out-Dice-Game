7's Out
i
  1. Players take turns rolling two dice to score points.
  2. Keep rolling until you get a total of 7.
  3. Add up the dice if it's not 7 and add that to your overall score.
  4. If you roll a 7, your turn ends, and you don't get any points for that roll.
  5. Roll a double (both dice show the same number), and you get double the points.
  6. Example: Roll double 5 (5, 5), and you get 20 points.
  7. The game goes on until one player reaches a set score, like 500 or 1000, and that player wins.
